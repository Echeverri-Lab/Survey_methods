# Pre-registration Go / No-Go Checklist

> Complete this checklist **before submitting** the preregistration and **before starting data collection**. Every item must be ✅ or explicitly justified as N/A.

---

## Section 1 — Hypotheses and theory

- [ ] Every confirmatory hypothesis is stated in a falsifiable, directional form.
- [ ] Each hypothesis is grounded in theory documented in `Step_02_Theory_Grounding/`.
- [ ] No hypothesis was written after preliminary data inspection (pilot data viewed ≠ data collection begun).
- [ ] The distinction between confirmatory and exploratory questions is clearly drawn.

---

## Section 2 — Measures and instruments

- [ ] All primary and secondary outcome measures are named and fully specified (item wording, response scale, scoring).
- [ ] All covariates and moderators are listed.
- [ ] Psychometric justification for each scale is documented in `Step_03_Psychometric_Scales/`.
- [ ] Manipulation checks (if experimental) are specified.
- [ ] Open-ended / qualitative items planned for analysis are listed, and the analysis method is described.

---

## Section 3 — Sampling and power

- [ ] Target N is derived from a power analysis in `Step_09_Power_and_Sampling/`.
- [ ] Minimum acceptable N is stated.
- [ ] Stopping rule is unambiguous (fixed N / quota / sequential with explicit rule).
- [ ] Inclusion and exclusion criteria are operationally defined (no vague terms like "poor quality responses").
- [ ] Attention check rules and data-cleaning decisions are written down before seeing the data.

---

## Section 4 — Analysis plan

- [ ] Every confirmatory hypothesis maps to exactly one primary statistical test.
- [ ] The statistical model (formula, covariates, link function, etc.) is fully specified.
- [ ] The alpha level and any multiple-comparison correction are stated.
- [ ] Assumption checks and their remedies are pre-specified (Section 8.3 of template).
- [ ] Pre-specified contingency analyses are written for foreseeable measurement failures.
- [ ] Exploratory analyses are listed separately and clearly labeled.

---

## Section 5 — Study design

- [ ] Randomization plan is documented (if applicable) in `Step_04_Study_Design/design_docs/randomization_plan.md`.
- [ ] Survey flow is documented in `Step_04_Study_Design/design_docs/survey_flow_diagram.md`.
- [ ] Any blinding procedures are described.

---

## Section 6 — Open science materials

- [ ] A plan for sharing (or embargo justification for not sharing) the instrument, data, and scripts is stated.
- [ ] Analysis scripts (even skeleton scripts) are version-controlled before data collection.

---

## Section 7 — Submission

- [ ] Preregistration document has been reviewed by at least one co-author.
- [ ] Document uploaded to OSF or AsPredicted and a time-stamped URL obtained.
- [ ] URL recorded in Section 1 of `preregistration_template.md`.
- [ ] A copy of the submitted document is saved in this folder with the date in the filename (e.g., `preregistration_submitted_YYYY-MM-DD.md`).
